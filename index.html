La misma forma arquitectónica en casi todo producto AI-heavy.

Mango closed the $3M seed with Ironspring in July, and BBVA Spark extended the facility the same week. The underwriting engine is reading real-time bank and document data and pricing credit decisions in seconds. The ops team is onboarding distributors as fast as it can absorb them. This page is a short read on where that stack will bend first, and where Eduba's work has helped teams in similar positions.

60%

Traditional code + Postgres

What the risk engine actually runs on most of the time. Deterministic. Cheap. Fast.

30%

Rule-based logic

Policy, thresholds, credit limits. Often dressed as ML. Rarely is.

10%

Genuine ML

Where the model earns its keep. Fraud signal, distribution shift, soft-dispute triage.

When a regulator asks how a credit decision got made, the answer has to hold across all three layers. The work is not to add more model. The work is to organize the context so each decision has a defensible paper trail.
